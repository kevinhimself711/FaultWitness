# I-0013 — Sanitized LangSmith and OTel Trace Foundation

## Status

Planned; depends on I-0009, I-0011, and I-0012. The available LangSmith credential is not consumed until this Iteration is authorized and I-0007 has secured it.

## Objective

Produce complete, correlated, sanitized, outage-tolerant Agent/model and operational traces with durable evidence outside backend retention windows.

## Scope

- TraceEnvelope, fixed stage taxonomy, correlation/causation IDs, allowlist sanitizer, and no-private-reasoning boundary.
- LangSmith Developer exporter plus OTel Collector to Tempo/Loki/Prometheus.
- Bounded encrypted buffer, idempotent replay, MinIO/DVC evidence manifest, and wall-time reconciliation.

## Non-goals

No raw prompt/secret/user identity export, LangSmith feedback feature, or claim about unimplemented Agent steps.

## Test and Eval

EVAL-G01-007 injects secret/PII canaries across all persistence/egress surfaces, forces backend outage/recovery, checks replay duplication, correlates stores, and reconciles trace duration with wall time.

## Pass criteria

- Missing required spans, secret/PII/private-reasoning hits, duplicate/lost replay spans: 0.
- Sanitizer rejection never sends the rejected payload.
- Root duration error is at most `max(50 ms, 5%)`.
- Candidate ends with zero pending production trace and at most 1,500 LangSmith base traces.
