# I-0012 — Authenticated Control API and Recoverable SSE

## Status

In progress after the I-0011 implementation and private runtime schema deployment completed on candidate `4844961`.

## Objective

Expose the complete frozen platform shell with authenticated tenant context, durable event projection, and recoverable SSE semantics.

## Scope

- All eight public paths, strict OpenAPI types, Keycloak JWT validation, two synthetic tenants, and four roles.
- Create/read/cancel/approval/feedback behavior over real state and Outbox transactions; truthful empty Tool and Skill lists.
- Durable SSE feed sequence, Last-Event-ID replay, typed retention gaps, heartbeats, and slow-consumer recovery.

## Non-goals

No investigation logic, real Tool/Skill capability, Action execution, public ingress, or production identity federation.

## Test and Eval

EVAL-G01-006 executes OpenAPI conformance, invalid/stale identity and role cases, idempotency conflicts, 10,000-event ordering, 100 reconnects, retention gaps, and backpressure.

## Pass criteria

- Eight of eight paths conform; body/header identity injection and cross-tenant success: 0.
- Same idempotency key with a different digest is never accepted.
- 10,000 events have zero loss, duplicate projection, or ordering error; 100 reconnects recover exactly.
- Retention gaps and slow consumers produce typed recoverable outcomes without blocking publication.
